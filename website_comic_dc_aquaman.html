Aquaman is the telepathic ruler of Atlantis and the Earth’s oceans, an Atlantean with incredible strength and speed as well as the ability to command all sea-life. His unique physiology allows him to survive on land and at the ocean’s greatest depths of pressure and temperature. He is a founding member of the Justice League of America.

First Appearance: More Fun Comics #73 (1941)
Creators: Mort Weisinger, Paul Norris
Powers: Superhuman Strength, Stamina, Speed, Reflexes, and Durability, Enhanced Senses, Marine Telepathy
