Batman is the protector of Gotham City, a man dressed like a bat who fights against evil and strikes terror into the hearts of criminals everywhere. In his secret identity he is Bruce Wayne, billionaire industrialist and notorious playboy. Although he has no superhuman powers, he is one of the world's smartest men and greatest fighters.

First Appearance: Detective Comics #27 (1939)
Creators: Bill Finger, Bob Kane
Power: Indomitable Will, Peak Human Conditioning, Master Martial Artist, Acrobat, and Marksman, Expert Interrogator, Genius-Level Intellect

Bane’s plan is to weaken Batman by forcing him to deal with the deadly villains one at a time. Batman becomes weaker and weaker as each criminal is put away. Eventually, Batman is taken to his mental and physical limits. Bane then makes his move and attacks Batman.
