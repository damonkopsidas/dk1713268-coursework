Left for dead in the Egyptian desert Marc Spector is found by Egyptians who worship the ancient Egyptian gods and carry him to their temple. Helpless before the statue of Khonshu, Spector’s heart stops. Khonshu appears to him in a vision, offering Spector a second chance at life if he becomes the god’s avatar on Earth.

First Appearance: Werewolf By Night #32 (1975)
Creators: Doug Moench, Don Perlin
Powers: Split Personalities, Expert Pilot, Skilled Detective, Weapons Master
