Fables was a comic book series published by DC Comics’s Vertigo imprint beginning in 2002. The series deals with various people from fairy tales and folklore – referring to themselves as “Fables” – who have been forced out of their Homelands. The Fables have traveled to our world and formed a clandestine community in New York City known as Fabletown.

Publisher: Vertigo
Publication Date: 2002-2015
Genre: Contemporary Fantasy, Dark Fantasy
Creators: Bill Willingham, Lan Medina
