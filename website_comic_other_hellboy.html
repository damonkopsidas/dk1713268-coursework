Hellboy was summoned from Hell as an infant demon on December 23, 1944 by Nazi occultists. Hellboy works for the B.P.R.D., an international non-governmental agency, against the dark forces of the world (and beyond).

Beginning with Hellboy, the Mignolaverse has grown to include many different comic series. This reading order contains all of them.

Publisher: Dark Horse
Publication Date: 1993-2017+
Genre: Adventure, Horror
Creators: Mike Mignola
